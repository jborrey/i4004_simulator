Instructions

  • 0  - halt
  • 1  - increment R0
  • 2  - decrement R0
  • 3  - increment R1
  • 4  - decrement R1
  • 5  - R0 = R0 + R1
  • 6  - R0 = R0 - R1
  • 7  - R0 = NCV
  • 8  - R1 = NCV
  • 9  - print R0
NCV = next cell's value. i.e. the value of the cell after the one with the current command.
